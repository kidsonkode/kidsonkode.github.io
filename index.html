The kids will learn basic concepts of programming and how the computer works. They'll build a tic-tac-toe game, draw simple animations and pictures.

We will use publicly available lessons which will allow the students to experiment on their own. Khan Academy, Code Academy and CodeHS are some of the sites we will use. We will also use some of our own lessons.
